# Implement Text Summarization for the given sample text

import pipeline
from transformers import pipeline
summarization=pipeline("summarization")
text='''Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming many aspects of modern life. It is used in healthcare to help doctors diagnose diseases, in finance to detect fraudulent transactions, and in transportation to improve navigation systems. AI-powered virtual assistants can answer questions, set reminders, and perform various tasks, making everyday activities more convenient. As technology continues to advance, AI is becoming more capable and widely adopted across different industries.
Despite its benefits, AI also presents challenges and concerns. Issues such as data privacy, algorithmic bias, and job displacement have sparked discussions among policymakers, researchers, and the public. Organizations are working to develop ethical guidelines and regulations to ensure that AI systems are used responsibly. Balancing innovation with accountability will be important for maximizing the positive impact of AI while minimizing potential risks.'''

summary_text=summarization(text)[0]["summary_text"]
print(summary_text)
